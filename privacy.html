MoneyOS Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 June 2026

MoneyOS is developed by AIIM SOFTWARES as A Sipuma Foundation App.

Information You Enter

MoneyOS allows you to manage income, expenses, accounts, debts, expected money, goals, budgets and other financial information.

Data Storage

Version 1.x stores your financial information locally on your device using SQLite.

Your information is not uploaded to AIIM SOFTWARES servers.

Notifications

MoneyOS may request permission to send reminders, budget alerts, bill reminders and financial tips.

Permissions

Future versions may request Camera or Storage access for receipts and backups.

Data Sharing

MoneyOS does not sell or share your financial information.

Security

Your data remains stored on your device. Future versions may introduce optional cloud backup and biometric security.
